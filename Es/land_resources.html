LAND RESOURCES

Land resources encompass the diverse types of land available on Earth, including arable land suitable for agriculture, forests, grasslands, wetlands, urban areas, and more. These resources are crucial for supporting life, providing food, shelter, and livelihoods, as well as regulating climate and maintaining biodiversity. However, they face threats like deforestation, soil erosion, desertification, and pollution due to human activities. Conservation and sustainable management of land resources are essential to ensure their long-term viability and to meet the needs of current and future generations.

Types of Land

  1. Arable land (suitable for agriculture)

  2. Forests

  3. Grasslands

  4. Wetlands

  5. Urban areas

  6. Desert and barren land

Importance of Land Resources


  1. Food production

  2. Biodiversity conservation

  3. Climate regulation

  4. Water purification

  5. Recreation and tourism

Threats to Land Resources

  1. Deforestation

  2. Soil erosion

  3. Desertification

  4. Pollution

  5. Urbanization

  6. Overgrazing

Conservation Measures

  1. Afforestation and reforestation
  2. Sustainable agriculture practices
  3. Soil conservation methods
  4. Protected areas and national parks
  5. Land-use planning
